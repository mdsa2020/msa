Letters of Love

Advancing Our Aspiration

Our Ambition

There really is no reason to wait when it comes to doing a good deed. The Letters of Love initiative is intended to serve as an easy-to-do and straightforward way to cheer up the moods of the patients through a simple, yet meaningful, handmade card. Send a long-distance hug with a heartfelt letter to those who can benefit from knowing that you’re thinking of them. 

We have partnered with the Children's National Hospital for this project and are taking letter submissions throughout the year. If you would like to get involved in this project and earn volunteer hours while doing so, make sure to check out our Get Involved page for more necessary information and the steps behind submitting/making your letters! Our work is not possible without your generosity!
