Everyone deserves to live a happy life

Learn More

Here at Medical Students Association, we know that sometimes all it takes to change the world is a little support. Since our founding in 2020, we have been determined to make an impact on the lives of patients in sub-acute, post-acute, and assisted living facilities across the US who suffer from a wide range of cognitive, neurological and respiratory disorders. The core of our efforts is to bring our team’s fresh ideas and passion to better assist the lives of these patients while empowering future medical professionals in their careers by providing opportunities to expand their passion for medicine.

WHAT ​PEOPLE SAY

"As we all know, COVID has isolated many of us and it was even harder for our friends who are living in sub-acute.  MSA has allowed our patients to meet and even interact with friends all around the world!  Buddies read stories, tell jokes, color pictures, swim under the sea, explore outer space, create music... all online!  Technology is so amazing.  Our patients would smile, reach up and clap their hands.  Those who are able to talk, had conversations with their buddies. 

We are thankful to be a part of this program because not only can our patients meet new friends, but the Buddies are also challenged to cater and personalize their visits with each patient based on their likes and abilities."

— Saratoga Pediatric Subacute Activities Director

WHAT ​PEOPLE SAY

"Every time that I’ve been a buddy, I have truly enjoyed it! Although all of the people I was paired up with weren’t able to verbally communicate their needs to me, I began to recognize the impact I was making through their subtle smiles. Something I’ve learned and enjoyed is ways to get out of my comfort zone to help others. As someone that aspires to be a healthcare provider, I feel like I’ve expanded my ability to support others. 

— Be a Buddy volunteer
